MEGA65 Welcome Guide
====================

Hello! You've reached the MEGA65 Welcome Guide. This Guide is intended for new owners of the `MEGA65 personal computer <https://mega65.org/>`_. It contains answers to common questions not (yet) answered in the official documentation, and recommends best practices for continued enjoyment of your new computer. It was written as an unofficial supplement to the MEGA65 User's Guide included with the computer.

I'm `Dan <https://www.dansanderson.com/>`_, known as dddaaannn#7325 in `the MEGA65 Discord <https://discord.gg/5DNvESf>`_ and `@dan_sanderson on Twitter <https://twitter.com/dan_sanderson>`_. If you notice anything in this guide that needs updating, or if you discover a topic not covered by the official manual that you feel new owners should know about, please `report an issue <https://github.com/dansanderson/mega65-welcome-guide/issues>`_. Thank you!
